Sounds Of Saturn Services

Here at Sounds Of Saturn we put our consumers first. We offer a variety of services to help you get the most out of your music experience. These will be listed below.

Food

We offer a variety of food options to help you get the most out of your music experience. These include: Burgers, Fries, Pizza and much more! These will be available in food trucks at the music festival.

Arcade Tent

We offer an arcade tent for those who want to take a break from the music and have some fun. This will include a variety of games such as: Pacman, Space Invaders, and much more! This will be available at the music festival in undercovered areas to make people feel more comfortable.

Rides/Games

We offer a variety of rides and games to help you get the most out of your music experience. These include: Ferris Wheel, Bumper Cars, and much more!

Booths

We offer a variety of booths to help you get the most out of your music experience. These include: Photo Booths, Face Paintings and much more!

Gift Shop

We offer a gift shop for those who want to take home a memory of the music festival. This will include a variety of items such as: T-Shirts, Hats, and many more types of merchandise!
